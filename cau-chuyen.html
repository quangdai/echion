Introduction

WTCO LEATHER GOODS - EST 2001.

Fashionable, trendsetting, and superb quality in every product are what WT has always been proud of and used them to uphold its leading position in manufacturing pure Vietnamese’s leather products since the 90s. With sturdy enthusiasm and excessive concern for Vietnamese leather merchandise, we undertake the mission to take interior products to a new height. WT proudly received the acknowledgement of Department of Intellectual Property of Vietnam in 2001

WT pursues the purpose to deliver to customers classical, elegant yet luxurious leather products. The endless extreme effort and the devotion of WT in years and years have been honored by both Vietnamese and foreigner customers, officially recognized as the most authoritative leather brand among consumers.

On behalf of the exceptional prosperous of WT, we have successfully spread our brand to the whole country and having stores in every credible Shopping Malls in big cities such as Trang Tien Plaza, Vincom Center, Lotte Department Store, Aeon,... comparable to world famous names.

Our engagement to make supreme products is not only the core value of WT brand, but also what makes the brand extraordinary. We are confident to be capable of satisfying each and every customer as well as ensure about the warranty services of every product.
